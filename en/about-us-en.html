About Us

Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use expense tracking application for families to manage monthly expenses and stay within a budget.

We are Kesar, bringing fiscal mindfulness to rural India, one family at a time.

Our mission is simple: to provide an easy-to-use expense tracking application for families in the neglected and rural parts of India.

Many have recognized the need for tracking family expenses; however, all the apps available in the market cater to urban, highly-educated users.

These apps not only assume digital literacy, but are often complex and only available in English. In other words, they are not accessible to a majority of the Indian population.

Our target users may not be able to read or write at a certain level, and may not be entirely digitally savvy–why should they miss out on the Indian digital revolution?

The internet connectivity across India is already vast, affordable and growing. Almost every family has some sort of smartphone available. With Kesar, that’s all they need to start their journey towards a more organized, digitally-supported lifestyle.

How else are we making an impact?

Our developers are engineers based in rural parts of India. We strive to provide learning opportunities to budding engineers who are eager to grasp the practical aspects of programming while being able to stay home and support their families. We hope that this model will encourage digital literacy and contribute to the development of these rural spaces.

How we’ll get the word out

The heart of this project is its users–that’s where our propagation strategy lies as well. We will be encouraging our users to teach others how to use the app, and in those conversations, encourage usage organically. Kesar is all about building habits, and we believe that the best place to pick up habits is from those close to you.
